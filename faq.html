Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe for kids?

Yes. We use parent dashboards, verified buyers, and clear community rules. No public contact details are shown.

Do I need to code?

No. Everything is guided. However, advanced learners can export their site and customize it.

What if I fail?

Then you learn. We review what didn’t work and try again with better data. Failure is part of the story.

How much time does it take?

3–5 hours per week is enough for most projects.
